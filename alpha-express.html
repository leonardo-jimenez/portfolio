ALPHA EXPRESS

Designing the first online investing brokerage of the Dominican Republic

Covid restrictions made life difficult for all the people affected by cancer, Cancercare needed to improve their experience to provide help and support in a closed economy.

UX Design
UX Research
Frontend
Backend

The client

Alpha is one of the leading brokerages of the Dominican Republic, founded in 2009 it has over +200M in assets under administration. Providing investment solutions to individuals and organizations.

Problem

The client wanted to offer current and new customers the opportunity of opening new accounts online and see their portafolio whenever they are. Due to strict regulation the process is complex and full of complications needing a lot of human interaction.

Dominican Republic didn’t have any brokerage offering opening accounts through the internet so we were the first facing this problem space.

Solution

Acting as an extension of Alpha’s team, we worked to research, design and iterate on many UX and UI solutions for both their main website, application and strategy for capturing new customers.

Limited Time

We had an agressive time to market and no experience researching the industry.

In order to work faster we had to select only a few tools to do “just enough” research and deliver the project on time.

We decided to work with the Jobs to be Done framework, Demographic Analisis and the Global Trends (benchmarking) of the industry.

Our Personas:
It’s all about the People

Originally the team provided two main personas for the users of the platform, the investigation showed an increasing amount of women looking for help investing with a very different outlook.

The data in the organization confirmed our observations on the market.

The Businessman

The mayority of current clients of Alpha, people with businesses or executive positions, with 50+ years looking to diversify their investments.

A lot of their thoughs are related on inheritance, how their assets will be passed to the next generation and in what distribution.

They think a lot about retirement and succesion.

The Rising Talent

Young executive rising through the ranks, normally in a medium management position and starting their family.

Frequently with a advanced degree of an university abroad of the DR.

Changes in their family motivates them to look for more security and ways to protect their assets and those that depend on them.
